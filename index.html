Our Story

From a humble grill to Port Harcourt's favorite

2020

The Beginning

Started with a single grill and a vision to serve the best Bole & Seafood in Port Harcourt. Our signature palm oil sauce recipe was perfected through countless trials.

2021

First Recognition

Featured in "Port Harcourt's Hidden Gems" food blog. We introduced our first seafood tower and started our delivery service in Egbelu.

2022

Expansion

Moved to our current location on Chinda Road. Launched the "Fire Menu" with 7 signature sauces and introduced our bottled sauces for home delivery.

2023

Live Grill Innovation

Became the first restaurant in Port Harcourt to offer live grill streaming. Our customer base grew to over 10,000 happy customers.
